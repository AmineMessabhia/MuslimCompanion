Our mobile app ("Muslim Companion") does not collect any personal information from users.

 

We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of our users' data. Below is a summary of our privacy practices:

 

1. No Collection of Personal Information: The App does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users.

 

2. Age Restrictions: The App is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

 

3. Notification of Changes: Any changes to this privacy policy will be communicated to users through the App, the first time they open it after the changes are applied.

 

4. Data Security: While no personal data is collected, we are committed to ensuring the security of any non-personal data generated or stored by the App on users' devices.

 

5. User-Generated Content: Any content generated by users within the App is stored locally on their device. Users have sole access to this content and can edit or delete it at their discretion.
